Research

Since its rough beginnings in the mid-nineties, online advertising has seen an explosion of popularity. In a 2015 study conducted by Helen Robinson, Anna Wysocka, and Chris Hand, it was found that creative characteristics of banner ads correlated in making them more effective. These characteristics include size, and a lack of intrusive text that the user may find annyoing. On the flip side, features such as animation and flagrant branding were found to be ineffective in generating clicks. Unexpectedly, it was found that long messages were associated with higher click-through rates (Robinson).

Big data analytics is the new standard in client and information acquisition. We believe that to stay with the times, incorporating some data analysis would be very helpful in making our app appeal to the most people. The article discusses three main techniques, big data, text and network analytics. Using these techniques gives developers new insight into what clients actually want, and what they find useful/attractive in an app (Lim).

Targeted advertising is the modern root to customer acquisition in the United States. Facebook offers businesses some of the biggest networks when it comes to advertising and allows each advertiser to gauge and apply which demographic receives this ad on their feed. This sort of targeted advertising is perfect for attracting new users to our service because it allows us to reach the specific demographics that show interest in our service (Kim).

The Entrepreneur gives companies seven strategies to make themselves more visible to the public on the web. Optimizing rich content and making the site easily shareable are just a couple of strategies our organization could implement effectively. This kind of advice is necessary for businesses and services such as DJFindr to remain relevent in an ever-incresingly digital business landscape (DeMers).
